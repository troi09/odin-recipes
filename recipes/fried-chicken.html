Fried Chicken Recipe

This recipe offers a delightful twist on classic fried chicken by incorporating calamansi, a small citrus fruit popular in Filipino cuisine. Chicken pieces are marinated in a simple yet impactful blend of soy sauce, black pepper, garlic and calamansi juice. The citrus not only tenderizes the chicken beautifully but also infuses it with a signature Filipino flavor that is both refreshing and zesty. Coated simply in flour and fried to a perfect golden crisp, the resulting chicken is juicy on the inside with a subtly tart and aromatic crust.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Minced garlic
  • Calamansi
  • Flour
  • Oil

Cooking Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into serving pieces.
  2. Marinate the chicken pieces into a mixture of soy sauce, black pepper, minced garlic, and calamansi for an hour or more.
  3. Coat the chicken pieces completely with flour.
  4. Heat the oil with medium-high heat to the point where it is hot enough to sizzle when we add the chicken pieces.
  5. Carefully place the chicken pieces to the hot oil and let it fry for 6-8 minutes on each side.
  6. Once the chicken pieces are golden brown, remove the chicken pieces from the hot oil let the excess oil drain.
  7. Serve.
